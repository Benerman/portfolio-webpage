    Capture Station

    #AppDevelopment #Python #NodeJS #UX
    #API #Applescript #FrontEndDevelopment

    The Lowe's Photography studio needed a way to streamline their process of tracking and capturing images. We leveraged an internal platform, Quickbase, to reduce cost and license fees. I developed an application that helped photographers scan a product barcode and retrieve all the required imagery for product in a shot list. Then connecting to Capture One Photography Software to pass the specific image name to Capture One through Applescript

    I was the only developer on this project, and I was responsible for the entire development process, from the initial design to the final deployment.
    Tools Used: Python, Eel(Python port of Electron), NodeJS, Electron, Postman, HTML, CSS, Jquery, and Javascript.

    US Dept. of Agriculture

    #WebsiteRedesign #UX #UI

    Our team chose United States Department of Agriculture's website to redesign. We redesigned USDA website because of its vast variety of information that needed organizational improvements for a better user experience. We conducted usability testing on the current design, and with our findings, prototyped a new layout. Through additional usability testing, we iterated on our original redesign to make it as clear and easy to navigate as possible.

    A redesign of this caliber is quite a large project. We barely scraped the surface of what could be done. Any Government website is a large undertaking, and we were able to make some significant improvements to the site.
    Tools Used: Figma and Visual Studio Code.
